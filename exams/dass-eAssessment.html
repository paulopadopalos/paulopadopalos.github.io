Announcements for eAssessment exams in DASS venues

Announcement 1 - As students enter the room

  • Please enter the exam room in silence and remain silent.
  • Sit at the desk number assigned to you and log onto the computer.
  • Revision notes or any other items must be placed in your coat or bag.
  • Coats and bags must be placed in the location indicated by invigilators.
  • If you have any writing on your body, please go to the toilet now and wash it off.

Announcement 2 - While students wait for the exam to begin

  • You are now under exam conditions and must remain silent.
  • Fill out your attendance slip now.
  • Once you have logged in, phones and smart watches must be turned off and placed in the plastic bag provided. The bag must then be placed under your desk.
  • Once logged in, identify the course unit space and find the exam link. Follow the link until you are asked to enter the exam password.
  • Place your student ID card and any equipment needed for the exam on your desk.
  • Any other items must be placed under your desk.

Announcement 3 - Starting the exam

Part 1 - All Students

  • Please listen carefully to the following announcements.
  • You must not communicate with other students during the exam.
  • Place your student ID card so it is clearly visible on your desk.
  • If your pencil case is not transparent, you must place it on the floor. Remove any items you need from it and place them on your desk.
  • Phones and smart watches must be turned off and placed in the plastic bag provided. The bag must then be placed under your desk.
  • Read the instructions for your exam carefully and make sure you follow them.
  • Any rough work must be completed on the rough work sheets provided. Do not use any other paper for rough work.
  • During the exam if you experience technical problems, wish to use the toilet or have any other issue, raise your hand and an invigilator will come to you.
  • It is very important to save your answers regularly as unsaved answers may be lost. You can change and re-save your answers at any time.

Part 2 - Students with rest breaks

  • If your DASS adjustment includes timed rest breaks, raise your hand to let the invigilator know when you wish to take one.
  • Rest breaks must be taken away from your desk.
  • After taking a rest break, a revised exam end time will be noted on your desk.

Part 3 - All Students

  • The exam duration is shown on the exam.
  • You will be told when there are 15 minutes remaining.
  • Make a note of the current time.
  • The exam is now starting.
  • Enter the exam password which is displayed.
  • You may begin.

Announcement 4 - 15 minutes remaining

  • You have 15 minutes left.
  • From now until the end of the exam, you are not allowed to leave.

Announcement 5 - Ending the exam

  • The exam has now ended.
  • Stop writing and ensure you have saved your work.
  • You must remain seated in silence until all paperwork has been collected.

Announcement 6 - Checking for any missed paperwork

  • Please raise your hand if anything on your desk has not been collected.

Announcement 7 - Dismissing the students

  • Thank you for waiting.
  • Remember to take your ID card and collect your personal belongings.
  • You may now leave.

Announcement 8 - Emergency evacuation

  • Please remain calm.
  • We need to evacuate the venue.
  • You must remain silent and not talk to each other.
  • Leave your exam papers and personal belongings at your desk.
  • Invigilators will guide you to leave via the fire exits.
  • Please leave in a calm and orderly manner and assemble outside the building.
  • You are still under exam conditions and must remain silent.
