About Me

So somehow you've stumbled upon this web page and it belongs to me. Hello, and welcome! I'm based in the north west of England (in fact my family has roots here going back at least 400 years). I spend my days working in higher education administration, dealing with student data systems and in particular have a lot of experience in scheduling and timetabling.

I'm also a born geek, and spend my time outside of work on various personal projects which you can find more information about below. My partner Mark and I are also huge fans of food and cooking, and you can see what we've been making recently on our Instagram account, Two Hungry Boys.

Projects

Combo Calculator

Combo Calculator is an iOS app written using SwiftUI to help universities with curriculum design. It calculates the number of possible pathways through a programme based on the course unit or module choices available to them, thereby helping identify the timetabling implications of student choice.

Exam Attendance

Exam Attendance is an iOS app written using SwiftUI for invigilators to take attendance during examinations. Attendance can be recorded by scanning barcodes or text on student ID cards, or by manually entering the student details.

Staff Data Collector

Staff Data Collector is an iOS app written using Swift UI to collect information about staff working preferences and availability. The data can be exported in UEF format for use with Scientia's timetabling systems.

Timetable Chat Bot

This was the world's first chat bot for helping students with their timetables. Written in Node.js and using NLP and ML, students could ask free form questions (e.g. "When is the next seminar for MATH101?") and receive an immediate response. The bot could be reached through multiple messaging apps including Facebook Messenger, Telegram, Viber, kik, LINE and Twitter.
