Exam Attendance

About the App

Exam Attendance is an iOS app which allows exam invigilators to take attendance at an examination in a number of ways. You can use the app to scan student ID cards for a barcode or piece of identifying text, and the identifier found will be logged. If scanning fails, or no suitable ID is available, you can manually enter a record for the attendee. The time of each entry is recorded, and you can optionally record the venue of the examination.

Once a register has been taken you can use the Export button to share it with other people – e.g. saving it as a file or including it in an email. The register is formatted as a comma separated value file (CSV) so you can open it in spreadsheet applications such as Numbers or Excel.

Data and Privacy

Your data is yours and yours alone. We do not collect any data from the app. All the information you capture through the app is stored locally on your device. When you choose to export the data, you can do so using the method of your choice such as saving the file to iCloud or sending it as an email attachment.
