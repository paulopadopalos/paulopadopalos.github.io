    Staff Data Collector

    Staff Data Collector is an iOS app for use in conjunction with the Scientia suite of timetabling software. It allows user to capture information about staff without the need for a computer running Syllabus+ or access to the internet. Once collected, data can be exported as a UEF file which a coordinator can load directly into Syllabus+.

    Download on the App Store.

    Data and Privacy

    Your data is yours and yours alone. We do not collect any data from the app. All the information you capture through the app is stored locally on your device. When you choose to export the data, you can do so using the method of your choice such as saving the file to iCloud or sending it as an email attachment.
