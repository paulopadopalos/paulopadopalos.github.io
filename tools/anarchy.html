    Anarchy

    Anarchy is my recreation of the classic 1980s game for 8-bit computers like the Commodore 64, Amstrad CPC and the ZX Spectrum.

    On each level you must destroy all the bricks – once you've done so the exit for the level will appear. But beware - there are creatures to avoid who are immune to your weapons, so timing and planning your route are key.

    The original version of Anarchy was coded by Michael Sentinella and released by Hewson Associates. I played and loved this game as a child, so recreating it seemed appropriate for my first ever computer game. You can play the original version at archive.org.
